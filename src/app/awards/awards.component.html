PADI Open Water SCUBA Diver
2011
As ironic as it sounds, technically I can SCUBA dive but I cannot swim. In recent years, for reasons unknown I have developed a phobia for being on the surface of the water. Nevertheless, many years ago, I, alongside other fellow young explorers, was sponsored by Mike Horn's programme to join their sailing and marine conservation expedition in Borneo. There, I had the opportunity to learn SCUBA diving.
